Avocado and Egg Breakfast Sandwich

Quick and Easy Avocado and Egg Sandwich

A simple variation on avocado toast, this easy to make fried egg sandwich is a great start to any day!

While this recipe calls for a bagel, any bread will work just as well.

Ingredients

  • 1 Bagel, sliced
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 1 Large egg
  • 1 Small avocado, peeled and pitted
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 Tbsp softened cream cheese

Directions

  1. Toast sliced bagel until golden brown.
  2. While bagel is toasting, heat butter in frying pan over medium heat and egg. Pan fry until yolk is set or of desired consistency, 3 to 5 minutes.
  3. In a medium bowl, mash avocado, sprinkle with salt and black pepper to taste.
  4. Spread 1 tbsp cream cheese on each half of toasted bagel. Top with seasoned avocado mash and egg. Add more salt and pepper over egg if desired.

Tips

For a refined twist, substitute bagel with croissant and use brie instead of softened cream cheese. Substitute fried eggs for scrambled according to preference.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with fresh fruit or pan roasted home fries and sliced tomato.
